Software Developer & projectser

I'm final year PhD reseacher in Particle Physics with a passion for developing data analysis software. My projects focuses on analysing thousands of terabytes of data from the ATLAS experiment at CERN. In particular, I search for detector signals consistent with a rare physics interaction between the two heaviest fundamental particles, the top quark and Higgs boson.
